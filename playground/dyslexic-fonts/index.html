How to get/use the Open Dyslexic font

Help for those who aren't so 'tech savy'.

There are two ways you can use this font on your computer. One is when you are viewing websites and the other when you are using computer programs.

1. Viewing on websites

Use this method if you want to change the font on a website to the dyslexic font.

2. Download and put on your computer

Putting this font on your computer will mean you can use it in programs like Microsoft Word.

Download this zip file (by clicking here), and save it to your computer.
